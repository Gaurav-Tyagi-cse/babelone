Our Services

At Babylon Engineering Solutions, we are dedicated to providing high-quality, precise steel detailing services that support every phase of your construction project. Our services encompass comprehensive shop drawings, erection drawings, 3D modelling, connection detailing, and rebar detailing, ensuring each steel component is crafted and assembled to exact specifications..

Our Steel Detailing Services

Our experienced team uses advanced industry-standard advanced software’s, to create accurate, detailed plans that allow for seamless fabrication and on-site installation. Every drawing and model is meticulously checked and cross-referenced with engineering designs, guaranteeing that our detailing aligns with project requirements, enhances safety, and minimizes errors during construction. We understand that precision in steel detailing is critical to the success of any structural project, and that’s why we are committed to delivering solutions that not only meet but exceed industry standards. By working closely with architects, engineers, and fabricators, we ensure that each project progresses smoothly from design to execution, saving both time and resources for our clients.
